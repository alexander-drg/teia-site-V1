ABOUT THE ARTIST

(The journey)
(01)

tEIA
bOCANET

   My artistic journey began as an exploration of materials and forms: I sculpted clay, wove stories into textures, painted emotions oncanvas/paper, and ventured into animation set design. Each stage enriched mycreative vision. For over 20 years, my passion for digital graphics has led meinto the fascinating world of 2D/3D art, where imagination meets technology tobring new universes to life. Over time, I’ve experimented with various techniques, from oil and tempera to acrylics, each contributing to shaping myartistic style.

    However, my inspiration extends beyond the canvas: I lovemusic, photography, and travel, which constantly open new perspectives andallow me to discover the profound beauty of the world. Nature plays a vitalrole in my creative process. It is an inexhaustible source of energy andinspiration, and the places I feel most connected to nurture my imagination, leaving their mark on every piece I create.
